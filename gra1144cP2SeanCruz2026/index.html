Strawberry and Banana Craêpes

Strawberry and Banana Craêpes with whip cream inside that you can make at home and enjoy. It’s fast and sweet to make, with ingredients from the store. Has adjustable ingredients to fit any person’s preference.

Ingredients

  • Melissa’s French Craêpes shells
  • strawberries
  • Banana
  • Nutella
  • Whip Cream

Instructions

  1. Take out a Melissa’s French Craêpe shell
  2. Spread Nutella in 3 straight lines from one of the shells to the other
  3. Clean strawberries then cut them with Bananas to place them onto the Nutella strips
  4. Spray whip cream onto the banana and strawberries the roll up the shell to finish the Craêpe and if so, desire you can put whip cream on top of the shell after it’s rolled up
